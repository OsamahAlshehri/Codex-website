Welcome to Health Codex Workshop 2024

Join us at the Health CodeX Workshop 2024 to learn about different ways to code and classify medical information, such as SNOMED, ICD-9, ICD-10-AM, Read, ICPC, and DICOM. You’ll also explore messaging standards like FHIR, HL7, Continua Alliance, and ISO/IEEE 11073. Meet with top experts, take part in interactive sessions, and attend hands-on workshops to improve your skills in health technology and coding. This event is perfect for anyone interested in health informatics, whether you’re a professional, student, or just curious.

Call for Abstracts

We also invite professionals and students to submit abstracts for the Health CodeX Workshop 2024. This is a great opportunity to share your research, innovative projects, and new ideas with a diverse audience.

Abstract Submission Guidlines
  • Should be relevant to the workshop topics: HL7 and ICD-10/ICD-10-AM.
  • Must be original, unpublished work and may include preliminary results.
  • Maximum 300 words.
  • Use a structured format: Introduction, Methods, Results, Conclusion.
  • All abstracts must be submitted by 12 Nov 2024.
  • Submit your abstract via the online submission link below.
